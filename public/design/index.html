10 — Voice & Copy

Direct, warm, embodied. Names what's happening without judging it. No emoji, no forced positivity, no gamification.

Nudge Prompts
Do
"Your heart rate shifted. What do you notice?"
Don't
"Uh oh! Your stress levels are up. Try taking 3 deep breaths!"
Insight Cards
Do
"On days you walk before 9am, your afternoon HRV is 15% higher than your baseline."
Don't
"Great job walking this morning! Keep it up!"
Error States
Do
"Can't reach the server. Your data is saved locally and will sync when the connection returns."
Don't
"Oops! Something went wrong. Please bear with us!"
Empty States
Do
"No signals yet. Notice is calibrating to your baseline."
Don't
"Nothing here yet! Start your wellness journey today!"
Response Vocabulary

Somatic descriptors, not emotional categories. The user names what the body feels like, not what the mind thinks about it.

Tight Open Heavy Light Buzzing Numb Warm Cool Restless Still
