What Notice Does

AI analysis finds the recurring signals in your physiology. Not generic advice — your specific patterns, connected to your actual experience.

Training, Not Tracking

Build the skill of noticing

Notice isn't a dashboard. It's a training tool. The goal is that you eventually notice without the app — not that you check it forever.

How It Works
01

Wear your watch

Notice uses Apple Watch sensors to continuously read heart rate, HRV, and motion. No manual logging. No check-ins.

02

Get nudged to notice

When your biometrics shift meaningfully, Notice asks a simple question: what are you feeling right now? Not what you should feel. What's here.

03

See your patterns over time

The AI layer connects your physiological signals to your self-reports. Over weeks, your unique patterns of bodily awareness become visible.
